What is happening?

Key idea: Lactase is an enzyme that breaks lactose into two smaller sugars: glucose and galactose.

A glucose test strip detects glucose. If glucose is present after lactase is added, that is evidence that lactase broke lactose apart.

GluGal + Lactase → Glu + Gal
